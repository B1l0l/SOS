What is SOS?

social battle
royale game

Each round, you and 15 other contestants compete to escape a deadly island filled with monsters. The trick is: three people can survive. Will you run solo or form friendships with others to escape?

Making the right decisions could be the difference between life and death .

What’s so special?

features

SURVIVE AT ALL COSTS

You have 30 minutes to find a relic, signal for extraction, and grabone of three spots on the rescue chopper.

CREATE ALLIES AND ENEMIES

Keep your eyes open for footprints and other indicators that help you stalk your enemies...

IMPRESS THE AUDIENCE

Along with your wits, you will have to equip yourself with various weapons found on the island. Guns are certainly an option, but melee weapons are just as deadly.
